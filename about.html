About Me

A personal reflection on my journey, goals, and experiences in the HNG program .

My Bio

My name is Eniola Awoleke — a hardworking, passionate, and creative individual currently studying Computer Science in my final year. I’m also part of AltSchool Africa’s Frontend Engineering track, where I’m building strong technical skills to bring creative ideas to life on the web.

My Goals In This Program

My goal in the HNG program is to challenge myself and grow beyond my current limits. I want to build real-world, user-centered applications, improve my teamwork and debugging skills, and prepare for full-time roles as a frontend developer.

Areas Of Low Confidence

Sometimes, I struggle with time management when balancing school work and tech projects. I also tend to overthink my designs or doubt my readiness to take on large projects — but I’m learning to take small, consistent steps each day.

Note to My Future Self

Dear Future Eniola, remember how far you’ve come. Keep learning, stay consistent, and never compare your journey with others. You are building something great — be proud of it.

Extra Thoughts
